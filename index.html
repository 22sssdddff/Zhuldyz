Top Hashtags and Content Types

Some content themes show strong links to high engagement. Hashtags like #Fitness, #Comedy, and #Challenge stand out across multiple performance levels, with #Fitness dominating high tiers.

Meanwhile, hashtags like #Dance appear more in lower engagement clusters, suggesting some oversaturation or variance in appeal.
